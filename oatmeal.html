Oatmeal

I once tried microwaving instant oatmeal.
Long story short, it took a while to clean up my microwave.

Ingredients

  • Some oatmeal
  • Water
  • Berries
  • Dried fruits

Instructions

  1. Toss oatmeal and water in a bowl
  2. Microwave your potion and hope for the best
  3. Clean up your microwave
  4. Decorate whatever you have left of your oatmeal with the berries, dried fruits, or any other topping
