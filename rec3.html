Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 squares of American cheese (may be substituted with other kinds of cheese)
  • 2 sticks of String Cheese (may be substituted with other kinds of cheese)

Steps to Success

  1. Put a String Cheese on a pan.
  2. Heat the pan until the String Cheese starts to melt.
  3. Put 2 squares of American cheese on top of the String Cheese.
  4. Wait for the American cheese to start to melt
  5. Put the other String Cheese on top of the American cheese.
  6. Heat the pan until the second String Cheese is fully melted.
  7. Enjoy your String Cheese Style grilled cheese sandwich!
