Peanut Butter and Yogurt

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of Peanut Butter (preferably Crunchy)
  • 1 cup of Beijing Yogurt (may substitute with regular or Greek, but YMMV)

Steps to Success

  1. Open the cup of Beijing Yogurt.
  2. Open the jar of peanut butter.
  3. Add 3-4 spoonfuls (or however many you want) of peanut butter into the Beijing yogurt.
  4. Bask in the glory of the non-Newtonian2 liquid.
