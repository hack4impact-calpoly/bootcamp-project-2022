Salmon Teriyaki

This is the best healthy-tasty combination: This dish features salmon (ideally alaskan but any will work!) drizzled in teriyaki sauce with a side of broccoli and steamed rice. This is a delicious, simple healthy meal that's perfect for weekday dinners.


Ingredient List

    Salmon
    Broccoli
    Salt
    Pepper
    Extra Virgin Olive Oil
    Teriyaki Sauce
    Rice


Cooking Instructions: (rice -> salmon -> broccoli)

    1. Rice: Clean the rice in a pot with water 2 times
    2. Fill pot of rice with water about 1 inch higher than the water surface
    3. Boil rice for 10-12 minutes on High, then turn on low for 45 min to one hour
    4. Salmon: Wash individual pieces of salmon and dry with paper towel
    5. Sprinkle salt and pepper and drizzle Teriyaki sauce over fish
    6. Bake on a aluminum foil for 20 min on 425 degrees F
    7. Broccoli: Cut broccoli into small pieces
    8. Place broccoli in oven at the same time as the fish
    9. Place all broccoli pieces in plastic bag and add olive oil, salt and pepper- Shake bag until mixed
    10. Bake for 30 min at 375 degrees F
