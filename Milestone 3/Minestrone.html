Minestrone Soup

A hearty vegetarian Italian soup made of beans and pasta.
It's sure to warm you up on a cold evening.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 diced white onion
  • 2 chopped carrots
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • Sea Salt (as needed)
  • Black Pepper (as needed)
  • 3 cloves of grated Garlic
  • 28 oz. diced tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups of white or kidney beans
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 3/4 cup of small pasta- elbow and shell most suitable
  • 1/2 cup of chopped parsley
  • Grated parmigiano reggiano (Optional)

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, salt, and several grinds of black pepper, and cook, stirring occasionally, for 8 minutes, until the vegetables begin to soften.
  2. Rinse, drain, and cook your choice of beans
  3. Add the garlic, tomatoes, beans, and broth. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in the pasta and cook, uncovered, for 10 more minutes, until the pasta is cooked through.
  5. Season to taste and serve with parsley and parmesan, if desired.
