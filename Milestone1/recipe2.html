Sweet Potato Fries

This recipe will teach you how to make crispy sweet potato fries!
A very simple and quick side for lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Size Sweet Potato
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 0.75 Tablespoons of Corn Starch
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cooking Spray

Instructions

  1. Wash and peel the sweet potato.
  2. Cut the sweet potato into 3/8 inch wide sticks.
  3. Stir together the salt, pepper, and cornstarch.
  4. Cover the fries in the salt, pepper and cornstarch until coated.
  5. Toss the fries with olive oil until coated.
  6. Place the fries in a single layer on a sheet pan lined with foil and coated with cooking spray.
  7. Bake the fries for 15 minutes at 400 degrees F or until golden.
  8. Turn over the fries and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until golden.
  9. Season with any additional salt and pepper to taste and serve the fries hot and crispy.
