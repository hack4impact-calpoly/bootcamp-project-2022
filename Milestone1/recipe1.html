Caff√® Latte

The classic latte perfect for quick runs while feeling energized for your whole day! One of the most popular drinks for college students to get through a tough day!

Ingredient List

  • 2 spoons instant coffee
  • Optional: 1 spoon sweetener of choice (I use sugar and can adjust to your liking)
  • Hot water (see Instructions for amount)
  • Milk of choice (see Instructions for amount)
  • Optional: Ice

Instructions

Iced

Note: A latte has a 1:3 coffee to milk ratio!
  1. First, add the instant coffee to a heat resistant mug and start boiling your water
  2. Then, add your choice of sweetener along with the coffee
  3. When the water is hot, pour the hot water into the mug to about 1/3 of the way
  4. You could either stir with a spoon or be fancy and use a frother wand to make your coffee mix look frothy
  5. Grab your favorite cup and add ice, pour your choice of milk about 2/3 of the way
  6. Finally, grab the mug with your coffee and pour it over the milk to see a beautiful gradient!
  7. Stir and enjoy!

Hot

  1. First, add the instant coffee to a heat resistant mug and start boiling your water and heat up your milk of choice
  2. Then, add your choice of sweetener along with the coffee
  3. When the water is hot, pour the hot water into the mug to about 1/3 of the way
  4. You could either stir with a spoon or be fancy and use a frother wand to make your coffee mix look frothy
  5. Grab your warm/hot cup of milk, the mug with your coffee, and pour it over the milk
  6. Stir and enjoy!
