Carne Asada Quesadilla

This recipe is a very cheesy and beefy carne asada quesadilla!
A very simple and quick lunch or dinner, fit for most occassions!

Ingredients

  • 1 Flour Tortilla
  • 1/8 cup Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 1 cup of Carne Asada
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Heat your pan over medium heat.
  2. Place your tortilla onto the pan once warm.
  3. Add 1 cup of cooked carne asada to one side of the tortilla and cover with 1/16 cup of cheese.
  4. Add remaining cheese to the other side of the tortilla and fold that side over the steak side.
  5. Allow quesadilla to cook for 2-3 minutes or until nicely golden on the bottom. Flip and cook another 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted and tortilla is golden brown.
  6. Serve and enjoy with salsa!
