Homemade Ice Cream

Good for summer or any time of year if you love ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Combine ingredients into bowl
  2. Fill cylinder of ice cream maker no more than 2/3 full
  3. Freeze acording to manufacturer's directions
  4. Serve immediately or store in covered container in freezer
