Bland Pasta

A pasta recipe I learned in 7th grade when I was bored.

Ingredients:

  • pasta
  • 2 ginger cloves
  • handful of spinach leaves
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 bell pepper
  • half an onion
  • green olives (if you like olives)
  • vegetable oil
  • aregano
  • love

To prepare:

  1. First, boil the pasta in salt water for 10 minutes. Depends how hot your stove is though.
  2. While pasta is boiling, we'll create our "sautee."
  3. Chop your onion, tomato, and garlic into very small pieces.
  4. Bring a pan of 3 spoonfuls of vegetable oil to a simmer on your stove.
  5. Add in the tomato, garlic, and onion. Cook all until golden/brown.
  6. Drain pasta and pour into pan. Start stirring.
  7. Add in spinich and olives. Stir until spinach is wilted.
  8. Add seasoning of your choice for flavoring.
  9. Tell the pasta you love it, pour into bowl, and eat.
