Recipe 2: Autumnal Soup

An easy soup dish that is fun for all the family!

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash
  • Pumpkin
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable Broth

Instructions

  1. Cut the sweet potatoes, butternut squash, and pumpkin
  2. Drizzle olive oil on them
  3. Roast them at 375Â°F
  4. Chop 1 onion and 4 cloves of garlic
  5. In a big soup pot, fry the onion and garlic
  6. Add the roast veggies
  7. Add vegetable broth
  8. Boil gently for a while
  9. Serve
