Cakepops

Like a lollypop...except cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 2/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • frosting (whatever kind you like)
  • 32 oz candy melts, coating, or white chocolate
  • Sprinkles

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  3. In separate bowl, mix butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract
  4. Combine dry and wet ingredients
  5. Pour cake batter into pan and bake for 30-36 minutes
  6. Once done, crumble cooled cake into bowl on top of frosting. Stir together
  7. Roll cake-frosting mixture into 1 tbsp-sized balls
  8. Melt the candy melts/coating in bowl
  9. Dip lollypop stick into melted coating and insert into center of each cakepop ball
  10. Decorate with sprinkles or other candies
