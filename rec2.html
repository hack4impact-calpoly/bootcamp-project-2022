Cucumbers with Ketchup

Ingredients

  • 1-2 cucumbers
  • Ketchup

Steps to Success

  1. Position the cucumber so that its length is horizontal; you should see something akin to a minus sign.
  2. Cut the cucumber vertically into slices.
  3. Open your ketchup container. If necessary, find a platform to place the condiment onto.
  4. Consume the cucumber and ketchup akin to how you would consume celery and ranch.
