Tomato and Egg: the only recipe on this site worth following because I found it on the internet

Kudos to MadeWithLau

I'm lazy most days so what's better than cooking the bare minimum.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tomato
  • 4 eggs
  • 5 slices of ginger
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup

Instructions

  1. Cut each tomato into 8 slices
  2. Thinly cut ginger and then julienne into thin strips
  3. Crack eggs, season with 0.5 tbsp salt, mix well
  4. Mix cornstarch and water together to make cornstarch slurry.
  5. Stir fry tomato with ginger, water, ketchup, salt, and brown sugar till tomatos are soft
  6. In a new pan, scramble the eggs
  7. Once eggs are close to being fully cooked, toss in tomato mixture
