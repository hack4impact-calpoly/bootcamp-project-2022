Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

My mom always makes this around Halloween. Very good treat during fall!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup pumpkin
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Blend sugar, butter, and eggs
  2. Add pumpkin
  3. Mix dry ingredients in separate bowl
  4. Combine dry ingredients with pumpkin mixture.
  5. Add chocolate chips
  6. Cook for 28 minutes at 350

Recipe Source: From mom :)
