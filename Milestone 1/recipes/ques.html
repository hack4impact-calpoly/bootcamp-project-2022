Bland Quesadilla

A quick and easy quesadilla for those who feel like eating something simple.

Ingredients:

  • 2 flour or corn tortillas (flour tastes better)
  • 3 shredded cheese mix from Costco (or any shredded cheese)
  • pinto or black beans
  • butter

Optional

  • guac

To prepare:

  1. First heat your quesadilla maker and add butter. If you don't have one, you can probably use a stove.
  2. Place one tortilla on and put cheese and beans.
  3. Place other tortilla on top.
  4. Close quesadilla maker.
  5. Turn over after you hear the beep.
  6. Take quesadilla out, add guac if you want, and eat.
