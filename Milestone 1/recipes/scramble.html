Scrambled Eggs

This is a quick and easy breakfast that anyone can make with eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp milk (just eyeball it)
  • salt
  • pepper (optional)

To prepare:

  1. First, crack the two eggs into a medium sized bowl.
  2. Next, use a fork or a whist to mix the eggs. Mix until blended.
  3. Add in a splash of milk. If you have added milk to the point where the color of the mixture is now a very light yellow, you have added too much milk. Start again from step 1.
  4. Add salt as needed.
  5. Heat pan on medium heat and put roughly 1 tsp of butter on it. Let melt.
  6. Pour egg onto the pan.
  7. Slowly mix the eggs in the pan with a wooden utensil. DO NOT overmix or overheat.
  8. Remove eggs from pan and eat.
