Neapolitan Pizza

A favorite classic inspired by recipes from Naples- the birthplace of Pizza.
Making it at home has never been easier!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Tipo 00 or all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp of instant or active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 1/3 cups of warm water
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 small clove of garlic
  • 15 oz. diced fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 3 oz of fresh mozzarella
  • 2 basil leaves

Step-by-step Instructions

Dough

  1. Combine the flour, salt, and yeast in a bowl or the bowl of your stand mixer. Stir to combine. Add the water and 1 tbsp olive oil and stir until a raggy dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface.
  2. Attach the dough hook to a stand mixer and start the mixer on medium-low speed, then allow the mixer to knead for 8 minutes.
  3. After the kneading is finished, divide the dough into 3 equal pieces. Using floured hands, gently shape each half into a boule (ball shape) by folding the dough under itself. Set each boule on a floured surface and dab the dough with a bit of olive oil to keep it moist. Cover all boules with a damp towel and allow them to rise until doubled in size, about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Transfer the dough to separate sealed containers, large enough for the dough to double in size again, and store in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.
  5. The day of serving, remove the dough from the containers, place it on a lightly floured surface covered with a towel, and allow it to come to room temperature before stretching, 30 to 45 minutes.
  6. Stretch the dough, making sure that it is not overworked. Use flour if dough is too sticky to work with. Shape it into whatever size and shape you desire.

Sauce

  1. Cut the garlic into a few rough pieces (no need to mince it).
  2. Place the garlic, tomatoes, oregano, salt, and olive oil in a blender. Blend until fully combine.

Assembly

  1. Preheat Oven to its highest possible temperature
  2. Slice mozzarella into desired slices
  3. Cover dough with sauce, and add the cheese
  4. Bake pizza for around 7 minutes (until the cheese melts). Add Basil, cut into slices, serve, and enjoy!
