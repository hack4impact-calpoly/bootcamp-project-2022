Hallie's Recipes: Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Mayo
  • Your Favorite Cheese

Directions:

  1. Heat up your grill to a medium temperature
  2. While the grill is heating, put mayo and cheese in the sandwich
  3. Put mayo on the outside of the bread
  4. Grill the sandwich until golden brown on both sides
  5. Enjoy!
