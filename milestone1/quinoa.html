Quinoa Fried Rice

A healthy quinoa fried rice that is vegan, gluten free and oil free. Made with wholesome, plant based ingredients, this is a nourishing dish you can enjoy for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1–2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 3/4 cups carrots, very finely diced
  • 3/4 cups broccoli florets, chopped
  • 1/2 cup corn (frozen or fresh)
  • 2–3 tbsp tamari, liquid aminos, coconut aminos or other gluten-free soy sauce alternative
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • pepper, to taste

    • Optional
  • sesame seeds for garnish
  • spring onion for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pan or skillet on medium high heat, pan-fry garlic until fragrant (about 1-2 minutes).
  2. Add veggies, and continue to cook until veggies are bright in colour and slightly tender, about 3-5 more minutes.
  3. Next, add cooked quinoa, and break up any clumps with a spatula.
  4. Add tamari, and stir to cook until well incorporated. Taste, and add additional tamari and pepper as needed.
