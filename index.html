Curry Man's Delights

Pani-Puri

A hollow, crispy-fried puffed ball that is filled with potato, chickpeas, onions, spices, and
flavoured water, usually tamarind mint, and popped into one's mouth whole.

Dosa

A dosa is a thin pancake in South Indian cuisine made from a
fermented batter of ground black gram (lentil) and rice

Tandoori Chicken

A South Asian dish of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices and roasted in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven
