Ingredients

  • Cup of instant noodles
  • Water (boiled in a teakettle)
  • A steady hand
  • Impatience

Recipe

  1. Boil approximately two cups of water in a teakettle.
  2. Place the cup of raw noodles, lid half-open, on a non-stable surface (ideally, carpet).
  3. Shakily pour the boiling water from the teakettle, being sure to burn yourself and waste some of the needed noodle-cooking water.
  4. Ignore all manufacturer instructions and wait for exactly 50 seconds (rather than a whole minute).
  5. Enjoy at an unreasonable rate, being sure to burn the roof of your mouth.
