Hallie's Recipes: Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 Tablespoons flour
  • 3 Tablespoons sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 Tablespoons mil
  • 1 Tablespoon oil
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 Tablespoon nutella
  • Powder sugar to top

Directions:

  1. Measure out all ingredients into a mug
  2. Mix all ingredients and top with nutella
  3. Microwave for 1.5-2 minutes
  4. Check with a toothpick that the whole thing is cooked
  5. Put powder sugar and/or more nutella on top
  6. Enjoy!
