Ingredients

  • Bread (ideally stale and a week old--why else would you make toast?)
  • A toaster
  • Forgetfulness
  • Butter straight from the fridge that will not spread

Recipe

  1. Remove the bread from bag, if necessary.
  2. Place in toaster and press down on tab, ensuring that the heat setting is too low.
  3. Wait until the toast is done.
  4. Notice that the toast is undertoasted and press down on the tab again to continue toasting.
  5. Find a distraction until the second toasting cycle completes, making sure to entirely ignore any odd smells.
  6. Return to a charred piece of toast, ready for buttering.
  7. Destroy this piece of toast with butter straight from the fridge.
  8. Ignore the mistakes; class starts soon.
