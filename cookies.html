Chocolate Chip Cookies

These always taste good

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup butter, softened
  • 1 Cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocoalate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
  3. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
