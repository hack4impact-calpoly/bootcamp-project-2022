Ingredient List

    1 cup butter
    1 cup sugar
    1 cup brown sugar
    2 eggs
    1 tsp vanilla
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    1 cup quick oats
    1 cup crushed unsweetened corn flakes
    1/2 cup shredded sweetened coconut


Cooking Instructions:

    1. Beat sugar and butter together until fluffy
    2. Add eggs, vanilla
    3. Add all dry ingredients
    4. Sift all together if you have time
    5. Add coconuts, pecans, oatmeal, crushed corn flakes and chocolate chips
    6. Stir to combine
    7. Line baking sheets with parchment.
    8. Using a 1/4-cup ice cream scoop, drop dough onto prepared sheets, about 3 inches apart.
    9. Flatten each ball with palm of hand.
    10. Bake at 350 degrees F for 8-10 min until golden brown
    11. Serve warm with a glass of milk. :-)
