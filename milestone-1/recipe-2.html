Blueberry Parfait

This blueberry parfait will satiate your morning cravings and leave you energized for the day! It is also great for an afternoon snack!

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Granola of choice
  • Honey (if you're feeling extra sweet)

Preparation

  1. Scoop desired amount of yogurt into bowl
  2. In a separate bowl, microwave your desired amount of frozen blueberries for 20 seconds.
  3. Add your blueberries to your yogurt
  4. Pour the desired amount of granola onto your yogurt
  5. Drizzle a bit of honey on top for the aesthetics (optional)
