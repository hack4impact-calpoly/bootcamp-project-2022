Mexican Flan

This recipe makes a very caramelly Mexican Flan!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Evaporated Milk
  • 1 Cup Condensed Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Essence
  • 6 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 Pinch Cinnamon Powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 347 °F
  2. In a pot, add sugar and heat over medium heat. Stir continuously until it becomes a caramel.
  3. Pour caramel on a flan pan while hot and reserve.
  4. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour into the caramel-lined flan pan.
  5. Place flan pan in the oven on a water bath. Make sure the water does not splash onto the flan!
  6. Bake at 350°F for about 45 minutes or until you can stick a fork in the flan and it comes out clean.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool. Refrigerate and unmold.
  8. Serve the Mexican flan cold.
