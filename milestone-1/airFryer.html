Air Fryer Chicken Toast

>>> Return to the home page

Description

  • Tender and Juicy Air Fryer Chicken is golden crispy outside and moist and delicate on the inside. This is bursting with flavor and so good!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Brine Solution
  • Baba Ghanoush

Method

  1. The Air Fryer Chicken Breasts already comes out super juicy so there is really no need to brine beforehand.
  2. You just need to make sure the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165° Fahrenheit.
  3. Place the chicken breasts and let sit for at least 20 minutes.
  4. Technically, since Baba Ganoush has salt already, you don’t even need to season the chicken with salt and pepper first!
