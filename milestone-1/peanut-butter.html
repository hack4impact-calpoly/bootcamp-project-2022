Ingredients

  • Over-toasted toast (refer to the burnt toast recipe)
  • Peanut butter
  • A lack of jelly

Recipe

  1. Gather burnt toast.
  2. Add a mountain of peanut butter to the toast with the intention of balancing with just as much jelly later.
  3. Open the fridge to find no jelly.
  4. Consume the peanut-butter-only sandwich anyway, sealing your mouth shut for all eternity.
