Camarones A La Diabla

Love spicy food? This is the perfect combo of sweet and spicy and is certain to appease even the pickiest of eaters. This rich meal packs a punch and will have you drinking glasses of water to get it down.

Ingredients:

  • 8 dried guajillo chiles, rinsed, stems and seeds removed
  • 3 dried chile de arbol chiles, rinsed, stems removed
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon coarse kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1.5 pounds large raw shrimp, peeled, deveined and tail-on
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Mix in bowl:

    Add dried guajillo and arbol chiles.

    Add very hot or boiling water until chiles are fully submerged.

    Cover with a lid or large plate and let sit for 15 minutes, until chiles have softened.

  2. Blend!

    Add the tomatoes, garlic, onion and salt.

    Puree until smooth.

    Taste and season with more salt, if necessary. If the sauce is too spicy, add more tomatoes.

  3. Pan on medium-heat!

    Add olive oil and shrimp.

    Cook shrimp for 1 minute per side, or until shrimp is light pink.

  4. ADD SAUCE!
  5. Wait until sauce is bubbling & hot!
  6. ENJOY!
