Description

  • Spicy Crab Roll is a popular Japanese sushi roll – creamy, satisfying, and fiery spicy.

Ingredients

  • The Rice
  • 1 ⁄4 Teaspoon of The Spice
  • The Sushi Mat

Method

  1. Getting the rice right is the trickiest part of making sushi. If you end up with enough rice for an extra roll, make an experimental one using whatever you’ve got in your fridge: leftover meat, herbs, etc.
  2. For ground cayenne, we recommend starting with 1/4 teaspoon, tasting the mixture, and adding more if you like.
  3. Though you can roll your sushi with a sheet of plastic wrap, we find reusable bamboo mats much easier to work with.
