Stir-fry Udon

This quick stir-fry udon dish will put a meal on the table in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of frozen udon
  • 3 bunches of bok choy
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 block of medium firm tofu
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 stalks of green onion
  • 1 slice of ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar

Preparation

  1. Chop the onion into slices, and try not to cry!
  2. Wash the broccoli, bok choy, green onion until the water runs clear
  3. Bring a pot of water to a boil
  4. While the water is boiling, chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces
  5. Heat up a pan with oil
  6. Sautee the onions until golden brown, and then add the vegetables
  7. Cook the vegetables to their desired softness
  8. Add the seasonings and add salt and pepper and sugar to taste
  9. Add the udon and pour a bit more soy sauce on top
  10. Plate it up and enjoy!
