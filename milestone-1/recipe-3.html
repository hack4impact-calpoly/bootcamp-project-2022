Fruit smoothie

This smoothie can be put together in less than 10 minutes and will quench your thirst in seconds! The sweet and tangy flavors are refreshing at any time of day!

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup of frozen mango
  • 1 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 2 cups of soymilk

Preparation

  1. Add the frozen fruit to the blender
  2. Add the milk to the blender
  3. Start blending on low speed. Slowly increase the speed until it reaches high
  4. Blend the smoothie until it is smooth and combined
  5. Pour your creation into a cup and enjoy!
