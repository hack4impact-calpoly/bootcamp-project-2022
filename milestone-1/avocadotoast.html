Avocado Toast

It's way too overpriced at restaurants so make your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • Bread (whatever suits your fancy)
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Fancy Salt
  • 2 Eggs

Instructions

  1. Cut avocado into slices
  2. Put slices of avocado on bread
  3. Put bread in toaster at whatever temp you normally make toast
  4. Make sunny side up eggs
  5. Once eggs and toast are done, place eggs on toast
  6. Dress with balsamic vinegar and fancy salt.
