Classic Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 2 persian cucumbers
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 oz feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 3 cups romaine lettuce
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. slice vegetables and add to bowl
  2. slice garlic
  3. combine bowls
  4. combine garlic, oil, vinegar in small bowl
  5. top vegetables with dressing, feta, and salt/pepper
  6. enjoy
