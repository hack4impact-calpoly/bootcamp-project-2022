Mom's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups butter
  • 2 cups flower
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 10 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pack vanilla pudding

Steps

  1. combine flower, sugar, pudding,baking soda in one bowl
  2. combine eggs, vanilla, and butter in another bowl
  3. combine bowls
  4. add chocolate chips and portion onto cookie sheet
  5. bake at 365 farenheight for 10 minutes
  6. enjoy
