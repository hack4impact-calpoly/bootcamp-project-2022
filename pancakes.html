Hallie's Recipes: Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Almond Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Tablespoons Maple Syrup
  • 1.5 Cups Rolled Oats
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:

  1. Measure all ingredients and put them in a blender
  2. Blend the ingredients
  3. Cook pancakes on a grill
  4. Eat them!
